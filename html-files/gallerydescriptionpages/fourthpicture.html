McLaren Senna

The McLaren Senna is a limited-production hypercar engineered for ultimate track performance.

Powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 producing 789 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque, it accelerates from 0–60 mph in about 2.8 seconds. With a top speed of approximately 210 mph, the Senna delivers extraordinary speed combined with immense aerodynamic downforce. Its carbon-fiber Monocage III chassis, massive fixed rear wing, and aggressive aerodynamic channels generate up to 1,700 pounds of downforce at high speeds. Limited to only 500 units worldwide and originally priced above $1 million, the Senna stands as one of McLaren’s most extreme and exclusive road-legal machines.
