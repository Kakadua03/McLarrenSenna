The McLaren Senna is represented by LEGO in the Speed Champions series as the set 75892, capturing the essence of this high-performance vehicle in a compact and buildable form. The set 42123 of the LEGO Technic series offers a more intricate and functional model of the McLaren Senna, featuring working steering, suspension, and a detailed V8 engine, providing enthusiasts with a hands-on experience of this extraordinary car.
