LEGO McLaren Senna (75892)

The LEGO McLaren Senna (set 75892) faithfully recreates McLaren’s extreme track-focused hypercar in a detailed 830-piece build.

The real McLaren Senna is powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 producing 789 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque, enabling a 0–60 mph time of around 2.8 seconds and a top speed of over 210 mph. This LEGO version features a working V8 engine with moving pistons, functional steering, and realistic aerodynamic elements inspired by the real car’s aggressive design. The original Senna is built around a carbon-fiber monocoque and was limited to just 500 road-going units worldwide. As a collectible, the LEGO model offers an accessible way to experience one of McLaren’s most iconic and track-dominating machines.
